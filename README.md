# English Improvement Plan (1-2 Months) 🚀

Ei file-ti apnar English reading ebong spelling/writing improvement er jonno toiri kora hoyeche. Apni protidin **30-45 minute** shomoy dile 1-2 masher moddhe khub valo improvement dekhte paben.

---

## 📚 1. Website ebong Resource Links

Reading ebong listening improvement er jonno nicher free resource gulo use korben:

### 📖 Short Story & Reading Websites
*   [British Council LearnEnglish Kids/Teens](https://learnenglishkids.britishcouncil.org/short-stories) - Khub shohoj short stories, sathe interactive quiz o ahe.
*   [English e-Reader](https://english-e-reader.net/) - Apni free account khule "Starter" ba "Elementary" level er boi gulo online e thik moto porte parben.
*   [Storyberries](https://www.storyberries.com/) - Sundor chobi shoho choto choto chotoder golpo, ja beginner level er reading er jonno perfect.
*   [ManyThings.org (Short Stories)](http://www.manythings.org/voa/stories/) - VoA (Voice of America) er simple English stories. Ekhane audio shune shune pora jay.

### 🎥 Best YouTube Channels for Beginners
*   [Easy English (YouTube Channel)](https://www.youtube.com/@EasyEnglishVideo) - Khub e simple dynamic animation ebong real-life conversation deya thake. Subtitle shune shune reading porar practice hobe.
*   [BBC Learning English (YouTube Channel)](https://www.youtube.com/@bbclearningenglish) - World class English learning content. Pronunciation ebong basic grammar shikhar jonno best.
*   [EnglishClass101 (YouTube Channel)](https://www.youtube.com/@EnglishClass101) - Choto choto rules ebong daily usage vocabulary khub sundor vabe bojhano hoy.
*   [Learn English with TV Series (YouTube Channel)](https://www.youtube.com/@LearnEnglishWithTVSeries) - TV series er scene dekhaye natural English, pronunciation ebong spelling shikhte sahajjo kore.

## 🛠️ 2. Helpful Learning Tools

Spelling vul thik korar jonno ebong sothik ortho janar jonno nicher tools gulo use korben:
1.  **Google Translate / Google Dictionary**: Kono word er pronunciation ebong bangla ortho na janle ekhane type korben ebong sound icon-e click kore sothik uchcharon shonben.
2.  **Grammarly (Free Chrome Extension)**: Apni jokhon browse korben ba email likhben, Grammarly automatically apnar spelling error gulo dhorbe ebong correction kore dibe.
3.  **Voice Typing (Google Docs)**: Google Docs-e "Tools -> Voice Typing" use kore English e porben. Apnar pronunciation thik hole docs-e thik word typing hobe, na hole vashabe na. Ati toiri korbe sothik pronunciation capability.

## 📅 3. Weekly Learning Roadmap (1-2 Months)

### 🗓️ MONTH 1: Foundation (Reading Pronunciation & Spelling Rules)

#### **Week 1 & 2: Loud Reading & Simple Sight Words**
*   **Daily Task (30 min)**:
    1.  [Storyberries](https://www.storyberries.com/) ba [British Council Stories](https://learnenglishkids.britishcouncil.org/short-stories) theke 1 ta choto golpo select korun.
    2.  Golpoti **jore jore (loudly) 2 bar porun**. Vuler voy korben na, na bujhle shei word ti notebook e likhe rakhun.
    3.  Notebook er word gulor bangla ortho ebong spelling check korun (Google Translate mathdome).
    4.  Protidon 10 ta simple word (e.g. *necessary, beautiful, foreign, structure, background*) 3 bar kore khatai/notepad e likhben banan thik korar jonno.
*   **YouTube Task**: Dine 1 ta [Easy English](https://www.youtube.com/@EasyEnglishVideo) video dekhun ebong subtitle ta jore jore podun.

#### **Week 3 & 4: Reading with Audio & Spelling Rules**
*   **Daily Task (30-45 min)**:
    1.  [ManyThings.org](http://www.manythings.org/voa/stories/) theke voice shune shune, tar sathe text ti porun (Audio-assisted reading).
    2.  Spelling basic rules jemon: **"i before e except after c"** (receive, believe) ebong double letter addition (run -> running, swim -> swimming) shekha.
    3.  Ager 2 soptaho-te likha notebook words gulo theke **5 ti kore sentence nije make korar** chesta korun (e.g. *I like beautiful flowers*).

---

### 🗓️ MONTH 2: Vocabulary & Active Writing

#### **Week 5 & 6: English Summarization & Diary Writing**
*   **Daily Task (45 min)**:
    1.  Akta simple news page (BBC Learning English app) ba [English e-Reader](https://english-e-reader.net/) theke beginner class er 1-2 page porun.
    2.  Reading pora shesh hole, **apni ja bujhlan sheta nijer vashay (broken English a) 4-5 line e likhun**.
    3.  Lekha shesh hole, text ta copy kore Grammarly ba Google translate e dia check korun spelling ebong grammar thik ache kina.

#### **Week 7 & 8: Vocabulary Building & Independent Writing**
*   **Daily Task (45 min)**:
    1.  Protidon 5 ti notun vocabulary sikhben (noun, verb, adjective) ebong notebook e spelling soho sentence create korben.
    2.  Daily journaling: Dine ja ja korlen sheta summarize kore English a 10 ti line likhun. Auto-correct off rekhe likhben ebong pore check korben check kore vul gulo mark korben.
    3.  *Pronunciation Practice*: Protidon 15 min jore jore text pore [Google Docs voice typing] a practice korun, dekhun system sothik word dhorte pare kina.

---

## 💡 4. Safolllotar 3 ti Golden Rule

1.  **Consistency (Niyoromito Practice)**: 2 ghonta ekdin practice korar cheye protidin 30 minute practice kora dynamic result ane.
2.  **Mistake Journal (Vuler khata)**: Apnar nijer spelling a ja vul hobe ta alada khatai likhe rakhben ebong soptaho e sheshe check korben.
3.  **Voyer Kichu Nei**: Prothome vul hobe, banan golmal hobe. Ati sobar khetrei hoy. Dhire dhire automatic apnar spelling memory improve hobe.

Good luck jahir! Apnar learning successfully start hok.
